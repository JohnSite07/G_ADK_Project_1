# Website Build Specification: "The Call of the Wild"

## 1. Goal, Audience, and Success Criteria

*   **Goal:** To create an engaging and immersive web experience that inspires users to explore and appreciate natural landscapes like forests, mountains, and lakes.
*   **Audience:** General audience, primarily individuals and families in urban or suburban areas looking for recreational nature-based activities.
*   **Success Criteria:**
    *   High user engagement with interactive elements (scroll animations, map).
    *   Click-throughs on "Learn More" and "Find a Park" links.
    *   The site is shared on social media as an example of a beautiful and inspiring website.

## 2. Sitemap & Content Outline

The website will be a single, scrolling landing page.

*   **Section 1: Hero**
    *   **Content:** Full-screen, auto-playing video of a stunning, serene natural scene (e.g., a misty forest morning, a time-lapse of a mountain sunrise).
    *   **Text:**
        *   Headline: "Answer the Call of the Wild."
        *   Sub-headline: "Discover breathtaking natural escapes."

*   **Section 2: Featured Locations**
    *   **Content:** A horizontally scrolling or vertically stacked gallery of 3-4 distinct locations.
    *   **For each location:**
        *   A high-resolution, captivating image.
        *   Name of the location (e.g., "Whispering Pines Forest," "Crystal Creek Lake").
        *   A brief, evocative one-sentence description.
        *   A "Discover" button.

*   **Section 3: Interactive Map**
    *   **Content:** A simple, stylized world or regional map with animated pins on key natural attractions.
    *   **Text:** Headline: "Find Your Next Adventure."
    *   **Interaction:** Clicking a pin reveals a small pop-up with the location's name and a link.

*   **Section 4: Call to Action (CTA)**
    *   **Content:** A clean, simple section with a final encouraging message.
    *   **Text:** "Ready to explore?"
    *   **Button:** "Find a Trail Near You"

## 3. Interaction Inventory

1.  **Hero Scroll Parallax:** As the user scrolls down, the hero video background will move at a slower rate than the foreground text, creating a 3D parallax effect that adds depth and immersion. This conveys the feeling of stepping into nature.
2.  **Featured Locations Reveal:** On scrolling into this section, each location's image and text will animate into view (e.g., fade-in and slide-up). This creates a dynamic, story-like unveiling of the destinations.
3.  **Interactive Map Pins:** The pins on the map will have a subtle pulse animation to draw attention. On hover or click, the pin will scale up, and an info card will appear with a smooth transition. This encourages exploration and discovery.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements

*   **Performance:** Page must be interactive in under 2 seconds. Total page weight should not exceed 3MB, with videos and images heavily optimized.
*   **Accessibility:** The site must meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards. All content must be navigable via keyboard, and text alternatives will be provided for all images.
*   **SEO:** Core Web Vitals to be in the "Good" category. Metadata will be optimized for terms like "nature trips," "best forests to visit," etc.
*   **Devices:** The experience must be fully responsive and optimized for mobile (portrait), tablet, and desktop displays.
